Who We Are

About Our School

Discover the story, mission, and values that make The New Promised Land School a place of growth and excellence.

🏫

A School Built on Purpose and Excellence

Our school is a vibrant center of excellence where character, knowledge, and faith are nurtured in every learner. We provide a safe, God-fearing, and intellectually stimulating environment that inspires curiosity, creativity, and lifelong learning.

Guided by dedicated and highly skilled teachers, we are committed to shaping well-rounded students who excel academically, morally, and socially. At our school, every child is valued, every talent is discovered, and every future is carefully built for greatness.

Admissions 2024/2025

We welcome applications from eager learners ready to grow. Here's how to get started on your journey with us.

How to Apply

1

Submit Application

Fill out the online admission form with your child's details and the class you are applying for.

2

Entrance Assessment

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a written entrance assessment and oral interview.

3

Admission Letter

Successful candidates receive an offer letter. Accept the offer within 7 days to secure your place.

4

Payment & Enrollment

Complete school fees payment and submit all required documents to finalize enrollment.

5

Welcome & Orientation

Attend our new student orientation to meet teachers, explore the school, and begin your journey!
